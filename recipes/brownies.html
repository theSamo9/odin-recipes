Quick and Easy Brownies

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

ingredients

  • sugar
  • flour
  • butter
  • eggs
  • cocoa powder
  • vanilla
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • walnuts

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 C)
  2. mix sugar,flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder and salt in bowl
  3. bake in preheated oven until top is dry
